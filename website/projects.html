Community Survey Project

After successfully creating my mobile app, I initiated a community survey project with a group of five friends. We chose to focus on raising awareness about the consumption of alcohol, its detrimental effects on health and family. Our campaign included educational sessions and a small awareness drive in the Seetharampuram area, West Godavari, where we engaged with the community to discuss these issues.


During the course of our campaign, I conceived an idea for controlling alcohol consumption through the use of an IoT device. This device would verify a person's identity using their identity card information and then regulate their alcohol purchases based on a set weekly and monthly limit. The goal of this system is to reduce alcohol consumption by monitoring and limiting the amount each person can buy, with the hope that this could lead to healthier lifestyles and reduced alcohol-related problems in the community.
